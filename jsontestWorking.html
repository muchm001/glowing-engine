Laminating & Coating

For over twenty five years, needlepunch fabrics made at the New England Nonwovens facility have been utilized as laminating and coating substrates. New England Nonwoven needlepunch fabrics are used in laminating process that include, wet adhesive bonding, dry web bonding, hot melt bonding, flame bonding and ultrasonic bonding. Needlepunch fabrics are also widely used in coating process such as, cast coating, expanded coating, extrusion coating, direct coating knife blade, direct coating gravure roll and direct screen coating. New England Nonwoven needlepunch fabrics are used to build composite structures where the needlepunch is used to provide bulk, body and resilience
