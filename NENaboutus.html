Mike Hochlowski and John Uchmanowicz purchased the Deitsch Plastic needlepunch nonwovens business in 2009. They upgraded the equipment and capabilities, broadened the product line and established the business as New England Nonwowens.

John brings 35 years of diverse experience in the nonwoven industry with an emphasis on plant management and equipment installation, design and upgrades. He has also been heavily involved with product development, sales management, TQM and customer focused results.

He has held several upper management position at major Nonwoven Business in the U.S. most recently running Deitsch Plastic's Nonwoven Division. He has expertise in numerous markets including automotive, craft, high loft, filtration, lamination and floor covering products.

Mike Hochlowski is a veteran nonwovens management and marketing executive. Mike was a partner at Globetec Nonwovens of Maryland prior to its acquisition by a larger company. Mike brings technical and marketing experience from a number of needlepunch nonwoven markets to include filtration, craft products, composite building and CIPP underground pipe repair liner construction.

The New England Nonwovens ownership, along with the sales and plant staff, are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products, technical assistance and competitive pricing to help them succeed in their markets
